Best cities to visit !

Rio de Janeiro

Visit the city that never sleeps, never stays still, where you could never get bored ! The most famous carnival in the world, Copacabana and Ipanema beaches, samba, caïprinha, colors, Christ the Redeemer, dance, music, sunrise... PARADISE !

Salvador de Bahia

On coastline of northeastern Brazil, it is a vibrant, cultural heartland that blends history, music, and natural beauty. Its colorful mosaic of cobblestone streets, colonial architecture, and lively markets gave Salvador its nickname : The "Capital of Happiness"

Manaus in the Amazon

In the heart of the Amazon, Manaus is a captivating Brazilian city that serves as the gateway to the world’s most biodiverse rainforest. A blend of culture, history, and natural wonder, and an unforgettable journey into both urban charm and untamed wilderness.

Nature

Lençóis Maranhenses

This stunning natural wonder offers vast white sand dunes and turquoise lagoons formed by seasonal rains. Known for its surreal beauty, this magical destination offers breathtaking landscapes, unique wildlife, and adventures like swimming and sandboarding.

Chapada Diamantina

Located in Bahia, is a breathtaking national park known for its rugged mountains, waterfalls, caves, and rich biodiversity. A paradise for hikers and adventurers, it offers stunning landscapes, crystal-clear rivers, and iconic attractions like Fumaça Waterfall and Pai Inácio Hill.

Pipa beach

In Rio Grande do Norte, it is a vibrant coastal paradise known for its golden sands, turquoise waters, and lively atmosphere. Perfect for surfing, kitesurfing, sunsets, beachside relaxation, and nightlife, it offers stunning landscapes, playful dolphins, and charming local culture.
